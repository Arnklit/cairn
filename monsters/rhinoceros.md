# Rhinoceros
6 HP, 2 Armor, 16 STR, 8 DEX, horn (d6+d6)

- Huge, armored, herbivorous mammals. Highly territorial, dwell in warm climates.
- With enough open ground for a clear run, it will charge its victims, enhancing damage.
