# Golem, Stone
14 HP, 3 Armor, 17 STR, 8 DEX, fists (d10)  
• 10’ tall animated statues carved from pure stone.  
• Immune to mundane attacks, fire, cold, and electricity.  
• Emits a magic aura that slows the movement of nearby creatures.
