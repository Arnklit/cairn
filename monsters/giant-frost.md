# Giant, Frost
10 HP, 2 Armor, 18 STR, 12 WIL, greataxe (d8+d8)  
• 18’ tall humanoids with blue-ish skin and pale hair.   Wear furs and iron armor.  
• Unharmed by cold and ice.  
• Excellent trackers, hunt accompanied by dire wolves.
