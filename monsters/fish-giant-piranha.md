# Fish, Giant Piranha
3 HP, 12 DEX, bite (d8)  
• 5’ long piranhas with colorful scales. Live in rivers and attack anything in the water  
• Dwell in large groups and overwhelm victims by swarming them  
 • Once it smells blood, it will not stop attacking.
