# Minotaur
6 HP, Armor 1, 15 STR, 8 WIL, axe (d10) or horns (d6+d6)
- Large, muscular humanoids with the head of a bull. Found in mazes and labyrinths. 
- Attack other creatures on sight, pursuing until they are out of sight.
