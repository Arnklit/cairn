# Elemental, Earth
12 HP, 3 Armor, 16 STR, 8 DEX, fist (d10)  
- Huge humanoid beings made of earth and stone.  
- Create small earth tremors with their natural strength.  
- Can meld into the earth and move through it as if swimming.
