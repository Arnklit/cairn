# Giant, Hill
8 HP, 15 STR, 6 WIL, great club (d8+d8)
• 12’ tall, hairy and brutish humanoids. Dwell in hills and plains  
• Low intelligence, relies only on its ferocity  
• Frequently attack settlements for food
